Auto Linker Pro

Auto Linker Pro automatically detects plain-text URLs on the pages you choose and converts them into clickable, underlined blue links. This helps you navigate faster without having to copy and paste addresses manually.

Link Behavior When enabled, clicking a converted link will automatically open it in a new browser tab so you don't lose your current page.
